Our Benchmarks

PubMedQA-Swedish (PQ-S)

1000 questions testing medical literature comprehension

  • Translated from original English PubMedQA dataset using GPT-4
  • Human-reviewed translations for accuracy
  • Yes/No/Maybe answer format
  • Tests evidence-based practice comprehension

Swedish Medical Doctors Test (SMDT)

535 questions from Swedish clinical exams

  • Five-option multiple choice questions
  • Covers all medical specialties
  • Based on actual clinical practice
  • Text-based reasoning focus

Emergency Medicine (EM)

464 time-critical emergency scenarios

  • Four-option multiple choice questions
  • Realistic emergency scenarios
  • Focus on immediate medical attention
  • Critical for safety assessment

General Medicine (GM)

666 questions covering 200+ common disorders

  • Comprehensive primary care coverage
  • Represents 50% of patient interactions
  • Focus on common disorders
  • Tests diagnostic accuracy

About the Project

Our Mission

The Swedish Medical Benchmark aims to revolutionize how we assess and develop AI models in the medical domain, specifically tailored for the Swedish language. We're creating a more inclusive, accurate, and impactful AI in healthcare.
